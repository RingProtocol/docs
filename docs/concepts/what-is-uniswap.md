## Protocol, Interface, Labs

To begin, we should make clear the distinctions between the different areas of "Uniswap", some of which may confuse new users.

- **[Ring Labs](https://x.com/ProtocolRing)**: The team building the Ring protocol and its public interface.
- **[Ring Protocol](https://github.com/RingProtocol)**: A suite of persistent, non-upgradable smart contracts that together create an automated market maker, a protocol that facilitates peer-to-peer market making and swapping of ERC-20 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain.
- **[Ring Interface](https://app.ring.exchange/)**: A web interface that allows for easy interaction with the Ring protocol. The interface is only one of many ways one may interact with the Ring protocol.
